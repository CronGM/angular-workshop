Show/Hide Content

Using the ngCloak and ngBind directives can prevent FOUC (Flash of Unwanted Content) issues. Check the HTML for how these are implemented. Once you start the Angular app the content will be evaluated and shown where appropriate.

ngCloak requires no value and works at a block level.

You can also show and hide content dynamically using the ngIf and ngShow directives. Check the HTML to see how they are implemented. Also, use the background inspector to see how they behave behind the scenes. There are important differences. ;)
